Step 2: Our test observation

At the beginning we understand what's every test talk about and what parameters we should take into our consideration from here

  • Bridging test we found that prusa can easily handle this test, but make sure that the setting is 0.1 layer height, speed 60 and temperature 215.
  • Stringing test and Sharp-corner came out nicely.
  • Tolerance test the measurement we get are consistently different than the actuals then this indicates that the nozzle of the 3D printer is wearing out, so we decide to make another more accurate one.
  • Overhang test we found that Prusa can easily handle printing up to a 45 degree overhang without any support.
