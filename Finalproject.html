First Station (Camera Gimbal)

The problem:-

everyday in our lives we use our camera to take a picture or to record a videos it sound so easy ,but actually it's not, all of us suffer from poor quality, hand-shaking videos so in the field of videography we use a any sort of stablizer to make sure our footage come up with good quality.

Stablizer is mainly a device that seperate the operator movement from the camera movement, but there is some issues that we have to face using a stablizer

  1. take alot of time and effort.
  2. Delay in the responce time.
  3. Cost a lot of money.

IDEA In Nutshell

So the idea was really simple why wouldn't I use the technology of Digital Fabrication to create a motorized camera stablizer

The idea consist of main two parts Mechanical Design & Hardware Design

    Mechanical Design:Using Fusion360 and 3D printer
    Hardware Design: Controller, 3Moters, IMU, battery

Forth Station ( Start the journey ...)

Empathy phase:-

The Empathy phase was about interacting with students and some of fab lab community members getting to know about their journey in the field of digital fabrication, their opinion in how to simplify knowledge and the problems they face in selflearning.

I came up with alot of insights

First in how to simplify your content

  1. the content should has a clear objective
  2. should be simple, clear and easy to follow
  3. organized in form of storytelling
  4. clear connection between therotical and practical part
  5. very detailed with all tips and tricks

Second in how to present your content (Platform)

  1. visually comfortable: Design, style, color.
  2. Flexible and Engagment: Learn anytime anywhere.
  3. Supportive and Encouragment: Allow sharing their work and giving personal feedback on their personal development.
  4. Interactive:Students, content, Instructor.

In case of digital fabrication we need to make it more related to a specific field show how it really related to our community and tell about it's impact on our lives.
